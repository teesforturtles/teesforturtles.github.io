~Fun Facts~

  • It is estimated that only one hatchling in a thousand will make it to adulthood.
  • Adult Loggerhead sea turtles can reach more than six feet in length and get to be over 2,000 lbs. They can get to be the size of a VW bug!
  • Sea turtle hatchlings are about 2 inches long.
  • Sea turtles are reptiles.
  • Sea turtles date back since the time of the dinosaurs.
  • Sea turtles can't retract their legs and head into their shells.
  • Some common foods for sea turtles include jellyfish, seaweed, crabs, shrimp, sponges, snails, algae, and mollusks.
  • Kemp's Ridley is the smallest sea turtle. It only reaches 30 inches long.
  • Adult male and female sea turtles are the same size.
  • Sea turtles can live up to 80 years.
  • Green sea turtles can stay underwater for as long as five hours. Their hearts can slow down so much that nine minutes can go by before another beat.
